anthropic 72.0/100

This is a charming dog portrait with good composition and lighting. The main issues are slight overexposure in the white fur causing loss of texture, soft focus reducing sharpness on key features, and a cool color cast. With targeted adjustments to exposure, sharpening, and white balance, this image can achieve professional quality while maintaining its friendly, approachable character.

  • Apply sharpening to eyes and nose area using unsharp mask (Amount: 80, Radius: 1.0, Threshold: 3) to bring focus to key facial features
  • Reduce highlights by -15 to -20 and decrease whites by -10 to recover detail in the bright white fur, especially around the head and chest
  • Warm white balance by shifting temperature +200K and adding +5 tint to achieve more natural, inviting fur tones
