anthropic 78.0/100

This is a well-composed portrait with excellent subject clarity and pleasant natural lighting. The happy expression is captured perfectly, and the background provides good context. However, the image lacks punch due to moderate contrast, and the composition could be tightened. The slight blue color cast from the sky affects the white fur's appearance.

  • Apply selective sharpening to the dog's face and eyes while maintaining softness in the fur
