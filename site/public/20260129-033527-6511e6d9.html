openai 78.0/100

Strong, charming pet portrait with pleasing subject separation from the dark interior. The main gains will come from a tighter crop, slightly brighter subject exposure, and cleaning/neutralizing the window frame so attention stays on the dog's eyes and expression.

  • Crop tighter to reduce the thick white window frame (especially right side and bottom) and place the dog's eyes closer to the upper third; keep the window edge as a framing element but minimize dead space
  • Lift exposure on the dog using a subject mask (+0.3 to +0.6 EV) and open shadows slightly (+15 to +30) while keeping blacks deep; avoid lifting the background
  • Reduce warm/yellow cast in the window frame and highlights: cool WB slightly (Temp -200 to -500K) and/or reduce Yellow saturation in HSL (-10 to -20); optionally add a small magenta tint (+2 to +5) if needed
anthropic 68.0/100

This is a charming portrait with good composition and emotional appeal, but it suffers from slight underexposure and cool color cast that diminish the dog's natural beauty. The primary focus should be on brightening and warming the image to make the white fur pop while maintaining the nighttime mood created by the dark background. The weathered window frame adds authentic character and should be preserved.

  • Brighten overall exposure by +0.5 to +0.7 stops, focusing on lifting midtones to better separate the white dog from the dark background
  • Warm white balance by adjusting temperature +200-300K to shift from cool blue-gray tones to neutral or slightly warm tones
  • Add subtle clarity or texture adjustment (+10 to +15) specifically to the dog's face and fur
