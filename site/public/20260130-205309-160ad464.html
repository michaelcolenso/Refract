anthropic 68.0/100

This is a charming candid portrait with good composition and pleasant bokeh, but it suffers from slight underexposure and soft focus on the subject. The white fur appears dull and lacks definition. Priority edits should focus on lifting exposure, sharpening the facial features, and improving tonal separation to make the fluffy coat texture more prominent while preserving the warm, intimate mood created by the ambient lighting.

  • Increase overall exposure by +0.5 to +0.7 stops, focusing on lifting midtones and shadows to brighten the dog's white fur while maintaining highlight detail
  • Apply selective sharpening to the dog's face, particularly the eye, nose, and whiskers, using an unsharp mask (Amount: 60-80, Radius: 1.0) or clarity adjustment
  • Adjust white balance by reducing yellow tones slightly (-5 to -10 in temperature) to achieve more neutral white fur while preserving the warm ambient lighting from the ceiling lights
