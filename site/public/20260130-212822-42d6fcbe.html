openai 78.0/100

The image has a charming candid moment with pleasing exposure and low noise, but the busy background reduces subject impact. A tighter crop, slightly cooler WB, and selective highlight control on the white fur will improve separation and texture while keeping the cozy mood intact.

  • Crop tighter to reduce distracting background (left doorframe and upper doorway) and place the dog's face/nose closer to a rule-of-thirds point; consider a 4:5 or tighter landscape crop that keeps the paws while minimizing empty wall space
  • Cool white balance slightly (reduce Temp a bit) and add a touch of magenta if needed; then selectively warm the highlights on the fur only if it starts to look too clinical
  • Recover fur detail by lowering highlights/whites slightly (Highlights -10 to -25, Whites -5 to -15) and use a small local brush on the brightest fur patches; add mild texture/clarity on the fur (+10 to +20) while keeping overall clarity conservative
