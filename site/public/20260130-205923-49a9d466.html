openai 68.0/100

The image has a charming candid moment and pleasing background blur, but it looks a bit dim with a warm indoor color cast. Modest tonal lift, slight WB correction, targeted sharpening, and noise reduction will make the dog’s face feel clearer and more vibrant while keeping the cozy mood.

  • Lift exposure slightly (+0.2 to +0.4) and raise shadows on the dog’s face (+15 to +25) while pulling highlights down a touch (-10) to keep fur detail
  • Adjust white balance slightly cooler (reduce Temp a bit) and add a small magenta tint (+3 to +8) to counter warm/yellow indoor cast in the fur
  • Apply selective sharpening/texture on eyes and nose (Texture +10 to +20, Sharpening masked to edges) and add a small clarity boost on the muzzle; avoid sharpening the background
anthropic 62.0/100

This is a charming pet portrait with excellent subject expression and composition. The main technical limitation is the soft focus, which reduces sharpness in critical areas like the eyes. Strategic sharpening and highlight recovery will significantly improve image quality while maintaining the warm, playful character that makes this photo appealing.

  • Apply sharpening to eyes and nose using selective masking, increase clarity by 15-20
  • Reduce highlights by -15 to -20, especially on the white fur to recover texture and detail
  • Increase contrast in the eyes by darkening the pupils and adding local contrast to create more depth
