openai 78.0/100

The image has a strong, engaging moment and pleasing background blur, but the bright white fur is slightly hot and could use highlight recovery to restore texture. Local contrast/detail on the face and a slightly tighter crop would increase impact without changing the candid, cozy mood.

  • Recover detail in the white fur by lowering Highlights (~-30 to -60) and slightly lowering Whites (~-10 to -25); add a mild midtone contrast S-curve to keep depth
  • Apply a selective mask on the face/muzzle: lift Shadows slightly (+10 to +25) and add Texture/Clarity (+10 to +20) while protecting highlights with mask range (luminance)
  • Refine composition with a tighter crop (reduce empty space above the head) and slight vertical alignment so the head feels more centered and intentional
anthropic 68.0/100

This is a charming portrait with good separation between subject and background. The main technical issue is underexposure that robs the white coat of its brilliance and a cool color cast that makes the fur appear bluish. With moderate exposure and white balance corrections plus targeted sharpening, this could become a striking portrait that showcases the breed's characteristic fluffy white coat and joyful personality.

  • Increase overall exposure by +0.5 to +0.7 stops to brighten the white fur and reveal texture detail
  • Adjust white balance by adding +300-400K warmth to shift from cool/neutral to slightly warm tone
  • Apply selective sharpening to the eyes, nose, and whiskers using a clarity or sharpening mask
